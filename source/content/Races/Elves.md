Race

The Elves were born before Men, and are wiser, fairer, and more powerful. They were also the main targets of Sauron's scheme during [[The Forging of the Rings of Power]]. They are split between [[High Elves]] and [[Silvan Elves]], depending on who journeyed across the Great Sea to Valinor and witnessed The Light of the Two Trees. Their Rings of Power have prevented their fading, but near the end of the Third Age and during the War of the Ring, they are fading and must pass the Sea to the West.

For the most part, they cannot die due to disease or old age, but can die in battle or of a broken heart. They are the most beautiful and fair of all the peoples of Middle-earth, and all have a deep and powerful connection with the world. In addition to perfect eyesight and dark vision, they are light-footed, making them perfect hunters and survivalists.
