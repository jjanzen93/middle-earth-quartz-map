People
The Harfoots were the most numerous of the three Hobbit branches. They were the first to move westward during [[The Wandering Days]], crossing the mountains and spreading through Eriador. They also made up the majority of those who first crossed the Brandywine during [[The Founding of the Shire]]. Since they were the dominant cultural force, much of their habits (like living in holes, eating large meals of bountiful produce, etc.) became the standard for all Hobbits.

Harfoots had a few notable characteristics when compared to the other branches of [[Hobbits]]. They had darker skin, were shorter, and could not grow beards. They were exceptionally nimble, which was useful as they preferred highlands and hillsides for their in-ground dwellings.
