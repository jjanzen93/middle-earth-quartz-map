People
The Men of Gondor are the inhabitants of the surviving Southern Kingdom of the Númenóreans. They were heavily involved in [[The Last Alliance of Elves and Men]], and now hold the line at the border of Mordor. Additionally, they think highly of the [[Rohirrim]].

Men of Gondor tend to be extremely proud and think of themselves as very disciplined.  They are great builders, like with stone cities and fortresses like Minas Tirith and Osgiliath. In the Fellowship, Boromir is their representative, as he and his brother had dreams about the events of the War of the Ring.

**Citations:** _The Fellowship of the Ring_, Book 2, Ch. 2: The Council of Elrond (p. 239-248).
