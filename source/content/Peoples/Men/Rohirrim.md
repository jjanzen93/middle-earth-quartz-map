People
The Rohirrim occupy an area near Gondor, especially a settlement called Edoras. They are good friends of the [[Men of Gondor]].

They are proud, bold, and generous, much like those from Gondor, though may be paying tribute to Sauron. Their society revolves around their mastery of horsemanship, which is what rumor says they are giving to the enemy.

**Citations:** _The Two Towers_, Book 2, Ch. 2: The Council of Elrond (p. 262).
