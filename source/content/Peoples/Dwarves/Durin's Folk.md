[[Dwarves]]

Durin's Folk had taken up residence in Khazad-dûm, and were responsible for [[The Fall of Khazad-dûm]]. Eventually, they moved under Erebor (the Lonely Mountain). Balin attempted to retake Moria (*see [[Balin's Expedition]]*), but was unfortunately killed by the Orcs that dwelled inside, along with the entire force sent to retake it.

They were responsible for the mining of *mithril* as it was only found in Moria. They are the most diplomatic of the Dwarf clans, which is why this is the only group of Dwarves listed on this map. This is also why Gloin and his son Gimli were at the Council of Elrond.

**Citations**: _The Fellowship of the Ring_, Book 2, Ch. 2: The Council of Elrond (p. 240-241); Book 2, Ch. 4: A Journey in the Dark (p. 311-320); Book 2, Ch. 5: The Bridge of Khazad-dûm (p. 321-323).
