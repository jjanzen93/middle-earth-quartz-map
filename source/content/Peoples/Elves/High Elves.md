[[Elves]]

Also called Deep Elves, they travelled to Valinor to see the Light of the Two Trees, which endowed them with a certain level of enlightenment. Despite this, they were decieved during [[The Forging of the Rings of Power]] by Sauron. The strength of those rings can be attributed to their excellent craftsmanship. In response to this deception, they formed [[The Last Alliance of Elves and Men]]. In the time of The Lord of the Rings, very few high elves remain (Galadriel, Glorfindel, Gildor, etc.), living mostly in Rivendell or Lothlorien.

They are the most powerful Elves in Middle-earth, existing simultaneously between worlds and as a result, possess a great spiritual power. Their eyes are described as reminiscent of glimmering starlight. In addition to their craftsmanship, they are exceptional masters of healing. 

Basically, they are the most perfect beings to walk Middle-earth, certainly more perfect than you [[Silvan Elves]]. They always have been and they always will be. Should have seen the Light of the Two Trees while you had the chance!
