[[Elves]] 

Silvan Elves are Elves who bailed on Valinor and never crossed the Great Sea. They tend to be more brash and less wise than [[High Elves]]. For example, they particpated in [[The Last Alliance of Elves and Men]], but acted somewhat independently, resulting in a great number of casualties. Some of them, the Galadhrim, used to live near Moria. After [[The Fall of Khazad-dûm]], it became far too unsafe, and they moved south.

They are much more secretive and sheltered than High Elves, preferring to stay within the forests and out of sight. This background means that they often wear forest tones like browns and greens, and are excellent at archery, stealth, and tracking. Legolas is their representative in the Fellowship. Specifically, he represents those of the Northern Mirkwood, not Lothlorien.

**Citations:** _The Fellowship of the Ring_, Book 2, Ch. 2: The Council of Elrond (p. 239-248); Book 2, Ch. 6: Lothlórien (p. 338-342).
