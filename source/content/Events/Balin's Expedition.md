Event
Some years before the *Lord of the Rings*, Balin, Oin, Ori, and many [[Durin's Folk]] tried to reclaim Moria. For a while, this was successful, clearing Orcs from some parts of the mine. The success inspired Balin to take up the title of "Lord of Moria", even managing to mine mithril for a while. However, soon the Orcs sealed them inside the mines, killed Balin, and ultimately killed the entire colony.

Seeing as they were dead, they did not do a very good job at recordkeeping, as this story is only pieced together with fragments of parchment that Gandalf is able to find while that same group of Orcs is on their way to ruin the Fellowship's day.

**Citations:** _The Fellowship of the Ring_, Book 2, Ch. 2: The Council of Elrond (p. 240-241); Book 2, Ch. 5: The Bridge of Khazad-dûm (p. 320-323).
