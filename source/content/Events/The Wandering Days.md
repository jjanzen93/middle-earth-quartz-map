Event
The Wandering Days were the period of time in which the Hobbits migrated westward over the Misty Mountains. The [[Harfoots]] were the first to cross the mountains, since they preferred highlands and hillsides and were thus used to that terraub. They were followed by the [[Stoors]], though they did not want to leave the Great River Anduin. Their love for water shaped their settlements in the future (shoutout Smeags). Lastly, the [[Fallohides]] moved along the River Hoarwell north of Rivendell.

They moved mostly because of the increasing numbers of Men in the East and the shadow growing over the Mirkwood. These three branches started to mingle and share cultural traits, settling in places like Bree. This event marks the moment when Hobbits became less of a scattered fragmented people and began to evolve into the agrarian, tightly grouped society that we are familiar with.
